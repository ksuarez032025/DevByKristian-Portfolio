Career Experience & Technical Growth

Hi, I’m a developer with a background in credit unions, payments, and problem-solving. With over 14 years of experience in the financial services industry, I built my career leading card operations, payments processing, and fraud mitigation at the credit union level. As the Card Services Manager at Empower Federal Credit Union, I was responsible for overseeing Mastercard credit and debit card programs, managing vendor relationships and product conversions.

One of my most impactful contributions came through close collaboration with our internal application services team. I served as a bridge between card operations and technical teams—testing new workflows, validating daily file generation processes, and ensuring API integrations were mapped accurately to the correct data points. I worked hands-on with system functionality, catching errors before they reached production, and helping translate operational needs into clean technical executions.

Throughout my career, I regularly collaborated with departments across the credit union, including accounting for chargebacks and daily settlement tracking, commercial lending (business credit cards), compliance, internal audit, and lending quality control. My passion for understanding how people interact with systems became my greatest strength: bridging human workflows with the technologies that powered them.

That drive to solve operational challenges through streamlined tech solutions is what ultimately sparked my transition into coding and development.

Now, I’m focused on front-end development through SheCodes, learning to build responsive, accessible websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. My background taught me how to identify process pain points and work across teams to implement system-level solutions and that same mindset now shapes my approach to development. From building user-first designs to integrating APIs, I’m excited to fuse my financial expertise with modern web technologies to create intuitive, scalable, and secure digital experiences that make an impact.
